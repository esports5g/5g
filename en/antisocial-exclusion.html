Basic Policy on Eliminating Anti‑Social Forces

Last updated: September 1, 2025

We will not have any relationship with anti‑social forces that threaten public order or safety. We will firmly refuse any unjust demands and take legal action, both civil and criminal.

Basic Policy

  • Cut off any and all relationships with anti‑social forces
  • Establish an organized and proper response framework
  • Cooperate with police, Anti‑Violence Centers, attorneys, and other external experts
  • Pursue legal action (civil and criminal) against any unjust demands
  • Verify counterparties and include appropriate contract provisions

Reporting & Consultation

If you suspect anti‑social forces are involved, please contact the local police and our help desk immediately.
