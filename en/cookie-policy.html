Cookie Policy

Last updated: September 1, 2025

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve convenience, understand usage, and improve service quality on our website. This policy explains an overview and how to manage them。

1. What are cookies

Cookies are small text files that store information about website use on your device, enabling settings retention and proper display on the next visit。

2. Main types of cookies we use

Essential cookiesEssential to provide basic site functions. If disabled, pages will not work correctly。
Functional cookiesUsed to remember language and display settings to improve convenience。
Analytics cookiesUsed for analytics to improve the site (aggregated without identifying individuals).

3. Third‑party cookies

We may use third‑party services for analytics, etc. Handling of cookies by third parties follows each provider’s policy。

4. Managing / disabling cookies

Your browser settings allow you to accept, reject, or delete cookies. Disabling them may affect some site functions。

5. Changes to this policy

We may revise this policy as needed in line with service content or legal changes。
